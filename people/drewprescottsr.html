Drew Prescott Sr.

 Since arriving at the VA after serving as the NYS CIO EPMO’s Program and Portfolio manager, Drew Prescott is now the Program Management Analyst for the Health Registries Program within the VA Office for Information and Technology EPMO. Drew supports the program manager and all projects team members throughout projects’ lifecycles and contractual obligations. Drew is certified as a Contract Officer Representative (COR) and FAC Project and Program Manager (PPM), and Project Management Professional (PMP®). Drew served honorably in the US Navy for eight years, and he earned his Bachelors of Science while on active duty. With extensive experience in project, program, and portfolio management, Drew continues to lead efforts in support of advanced governance while supporting the VA CIO’s Strategic Plan.. Drew is a disabled Veteran, thus providing attributes that fuel his desire to serve the VA with a strong purpose of excellence. He believes strongly in the noble and critical mission of the Veterans Affairs. Above all, Drew is highly regarded for his excellent networking and communication skills.
