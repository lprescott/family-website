About

I am a undergraduate at SUNY Albany pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics. Currently my interests have me implementing simple machine learning techniques -- various mathematical and visual libraries -- projects leading to full-stack development; see my GitHub.

I found my true passion in mathematics. However, while programming, I explored how to use mathematical ideas practically: the connections between the two topics led me to knowing this is what I wanted to do with my life! I love the combination of creativity and logical problem-solving skills I get to use when writing code; I love the real world applications and driven style of mathematics. I love how there is always more to learn!
