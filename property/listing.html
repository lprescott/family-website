Outside

The antique brick, bullseye glass, slate front steps and historic fireplaces make this home truly original. Plantings are mature and provide color and interest throughout the seasons. Whether you are admiring the Japanese maples, myriad colorful lilies or evergreens, you will feel a sense of peace and contentment with no one around to ask you to turn down the music playing on your large deck. Or, listen to the birds who call this property their home and love the fact that no pesticides are used. This is a safe, healthy environment for people, pets and wildlife.


Location

Our home is 2 minutes to the Mohawk River bike path, ten minutes to the Albany International airport, and close to many shopping centers.
